---
title: Delete Mode
description: Remove conveyors and racks from the layout
---

![Delete icon](../../../assets/icons/delete_icon.png)

Delete removes **conveyors** and **racks** from your model. **Links** between devices are not removed automatically — reconnect or clean them up in [Linking Mode](/reference/linkingmode/) after deleting devices.

## Keyboard

1. Select one or more devices (click, Shift+click, or drag a selection box on the canvas).
2. Press **Delete** or **Backspace**.

All selected conveyors and racks are removed in one step. Undo with **⌘/Ctrl+Z**.

Editor mode only; not while simulation is running; not when focus is in a text field.

## Toolbar

The **Delete** icon in the toolbar deletes the **primary** selected device (the first in the selection). For multiple devices, use **Delete** or **Backspace** on the keyboard.
